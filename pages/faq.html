Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our airport transfer services

Booking & Reservations

How do I book an airport transfer?
You can book through our website by clicking "Book Transfer", using our mobile app, or calling us at +251 91 155 0001. Simply enter your flight details, select your vehicle, and confirm your booking. You'll receive instant confirmation via email and SMS.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee availability. However, we also accept last-minute bookings subject to vehicle availability. For peak travel seasons, booking 2-3 days ahead is advised.
Can I modify or cancel my booking?
Yes! You can modify or cancel your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time for free. Changes made within 2 hours may incur a fee. To modify, visit "My Tickets" on our website or contact our support team.
Do you offer round-trip bookings?
Yes, you can book both airport pickup (arrival) and drop-off (departure) transfers. Simply make two separate bookings or contact us to arrange a round-trip package with potential discounts.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept multiple payment methods: Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Telebirr mobile money, and cash payment to the driver. Corporate clients can also pay via monthly invoice.
Is the price fixed or metered?
All our prices are fixed and confirmed at the time of booking. Unlike metered taxis, you'll know exactly what you'll pay before your trip. The price includes all tolls, taxes, and up to 30 minutes of free waiting time for airport pickups.
Are there any hidden fees?
No hidden fees! The price you see is the price you pay. Additional charges only apply for: waiting time beyond 30 minutes (500 ETB per 30 min), extra stops not in original booking, or late-night surcharge (10PM-6AM, +15%).
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. Cancellations made more than 24 hours before pickup receive a full refund. Cancellations 2-24 hours before receive 50% refund. No refund for cancellations less than 2 hours before pickup. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Service & Vehicles

How does flight tracking work?
We automatically monitor your flight status in real-time. If your flight is delayed or arrives early, we adjust your pickup time accordingly at no extra charge. Your driver will be there when you land, not when your flight was scheduled.
How will I find my driver at the airport?
Your driver will be waiting in the arrivals hall holding a sign with your name. You'll also receive driver details (name, phone, vehicle plate) via SMS before arrival. You can track your driver's location in real-time through our app.
What vehicle types do you offer?
We offer 4 vehicle classes: Standard Sedan (1-2 passengers), Executive Sedan (1-3 passengers), SUV/Minivan (4-6 passengers), and Luxury Class (1-3 passengers, premium vehicles). All vehicles are air-conditioned, clean, and well-maintained.
Do you provide child seats?
Yes! Child seats are available upon request for an additional $5. Please select this option during booking or inform us at least 24 hours before your trip so we can ensure availability.
How much luggage can I bring?
Standard and Executive sedans accommodate 2-3 large suitcases plus carry-ons. SUVs can handle 4-6 large suitcases. If you have excess luggage, please mention it during booking so we can arrange an appropriate vehicle.

Corporate Accounts

Do you offer corporate accounts?
Yes! We offer corporate accounts for businesses, NGOs, embassies, and hotels. Benefits include volume discounts, monthly invoicing, dedicated account manager, priority booking, and detailed reporting.
How do I set up a corporate account?
Contact our corporate team at corporate@shuttleplus.et or call +251 91 155 0001. We'll schedule a brief consultation, provide a customized quote, and set up your account within 24-48 hours.
